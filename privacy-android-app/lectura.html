Privacy Policy

The purpose of this privacy policy is to help you understand how we use our customer’s information.
By using this app you consent the app to access several subsystems of your device.
More generally, we think of our privacy policy as a feature and hope that you’ll agree.

What data do we collect?
No information is collected/organized from your phone, and no information is sent to any server.

Android Permissions
We request the following android permissions for the following reasons:
  1. android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE in order to read and write the words tables in your device
  2. android.permission.INTERNET in order to download the advertisements
  3. android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE in order to download the advertisements
  4. android.permission.ACCESS_LOCATION_EXTRA_COMMANDS in order to download the advertisements
  5. android.permission.ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION in order to download the advertisements
  6. android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO in order to access the sound system
  7. android.permission.MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS in order to reproduce sounds

Personal Information Security
We do not access nor collect any information about the user. More over, we take reasonable steps to help protect your personal information in an effort to prevent loss and misuse as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration and destruction.

Changes to this Policy
We may change this Policy from time to time. If we make any changes to this Policy, we will change the "Last Updated" date below. After such changes have been published to our website, your continued use of this app will constitute your acceptance of such revised policy.

We will not disclose your personally identifiable data to third parties.

Children
